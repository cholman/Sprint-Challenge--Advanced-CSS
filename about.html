About SpaceWalkers

Originally partnered with NASA in 1970 (the year of disco), we have since grown into our own space tourism business. Since 2020, we have safely launched 6 civilian space walks per year. We are planning to launch our first moon vacation program in 2028.

NASA Partnership

Our goal is to bring down the costs of space exploration. 10% of our profits go directly to NASA to fund public research. In addition we are doing our own market research to make SpaceWalks accessible to everyone.

With our help, we expect NASA to reach Mars by 2035. Following that successful mission, we plan to have our first Mars vacation by 2040.
